STUDY DRILLS

For these next few exercises, you will have the exact same Study Drills.

  1. Go back through and write a comment on what each line does.
  2. Read each one backward or out loud to find your errors.
  3. From now on, when you make mistakes, write down on a piece of paper what kind of mistake you made.
    4. When you go to the next exercise, look at the last mistakes you made and try not to make in this new one.
  4. Remember that everyone makes mistakes. Programmers are like magicians who like everyone to think they are perfect and never wrong, but it's all an act. They make mistakes all the time.
